Agribot

Overview

Designed an autonomous agricultural robot for robotics competition. The system consists of a skid-steering mobile base, belt-driven transmission and a robotic manipulator for agricultural operations.

  • Motor selection

  • Timing belt transmission design
  • Chassis CAD modelling
  • Bearing selection
  • Mechanical assembly planning

Tools Used

  • ANSYS
  • MATLAB
  • Mechanical Design

Outcome

Successfully designed the robot chassis and drivetrain satisfying competition requirements for terrain navigation.
